SNSF-COST Synergy Story

The Dual Leadership Advantage

Prof. Joerg Osterrieder serves as both SNSF Project Principal Investigator and COST Action Chair, creating a unique bridge between national research funding and pan-European collaboration. This dual role enabled the SNSF project to leverage an existing network of 420+ researchers across 55 countries.

What SNSF Gained from COST

  • Pre-existing network of 420+ researchers
  • PhD training via COST FinAI PhD School 2024
  • Conference platform (7th/8th European Conferences at BFH)
  • Joint publications with 30+ COST co-authors
  • Industry connections (Deutsche Borse, Quoniam)

What SNSF Contributed to COST

  • Leadership as Action Chair (2020-2024)
  • Conference hosting in Bern (2023, 2024)
  • TOPol framework and HFT classification methods
  • Bridge to MSCA Industrial Doctoral Network (EUR 4.5M)
  • Research case studies for PhD training
