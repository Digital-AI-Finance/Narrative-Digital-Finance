Statistical Significance Note

While NarrativeBreak shows economically meaningful improvements, the Ledoit-Wolf bootstrap test yields p = 0.034 (raw) and p = 0.17 after Bonferroni correction for 6 comparisons. We characterize these findings as "suggestive evidence" warranting further validation.
