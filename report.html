1.1 Achievement of Research Objectives

The project successfully achieved all three primary research objectives, producing significant contributions to the field of narrative digital finance and financial market analysis.

Aim 1: Econometric Validation

Validated and refined existing econometric models using real-world financial data from P2P lending platforms (Bondora, Lending Club) and high-frequency trading data from Deutsche Borse.

Aim 2: Narrative Analysis

Developed frameworks integrating NLP and narrative analysis to understand market behaviors, including multimodal approaches using text, speech, and multimedia data.

Aim 3: AI/ML Framework

Created multidimensional AI and ML frameworks combining network topology analysis with traditional credit risk features, significantly improving prediction accuracy.

1.2 Challenges and Outcomes

The project was completed successfully with all objectives achieved. Key adaptations during the project:

Methodological Evolution

The original focus on structural breaks and bubble detection was extended to include network-based credit risk analysis, which proved highly successful in P2P lending contexts. This adaptation resulted in two high-impact journal publications.

Data Access

Access to comprehensive P2P lending data (Bondora, Lending Club) and collaboration with Deutsche Borse for HFT data exceeded initial expectations, enabling more rigorous empirical validation.

Research Network Synergies

Integration with COST Action CA19130 and MSCA Digital Finance network created unexpected opportunities for large-scale collaborative research across 49 countries.

1.3 Contribution to Knowledge Advancement

Key Scientific Contribution

This research bridges the gap between traditional econometric approaches and modern AI techniques, providing new insights into the role of narratives and quantitative data in financial decision-making.

Novel Methodological Contributions

Network Topology for Credit Risk

First comprehensive study showing that borrower network centrality significantly improves P2P lending default prediction. Network features (degree centrality, clustering) enhance ML model accuracy.

Multimodal Financial Analysis

Novel framework extending traditional NLP to include non-textual data (images, video, audio) for financial instrument pricing using Generative AI.

Narrative Finance Framework

Established foundational framework for understanding how narratives shape financial markets, with applications to sustainable finance, AI, and emerging markets.

HFT Market Microstructure

Joint research with Deutsche Borse on reaction times to macro-news, contributing to understanding of high-frequency trading dynamics.

Impact Areas

Risk Management Asset Pricing Trading Strategies Regulatory Policy P2P Lending Credit Risk Market Microstructure
