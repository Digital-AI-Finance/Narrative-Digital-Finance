Dear Editors,

I am submitting the manuscript entitled "An Algorithmic Framework for Systematic Literature Reviews: A Case Study for Financial Narratives" for consideration for publication in Financial Innovation. The manuscript introduces a scalable and reproducible framework for conducting systematic literature reviews using Transformer-based text embedding and unsupervised learning techniques. It is applied to the domain of financial narratives, with a focus on the intersection between Natural Language Processing (NLP) methods and financial market dynamics.

The study addresses the growing volume and heterogeneity of literature on financial narratives by proposing a structured selection methodology. Using a combination of sentence-transformer models, cosine similarity scoring, Principal Component Analysis, and various clustering methods, the framework allows for objective inclusion/exclusion of papers and enhances the reproducibility of the review process. The application of this approach to financial narrative modeling identifies methodological trends, data sources, NLP techniques used in existing studies, and areas for further research.
