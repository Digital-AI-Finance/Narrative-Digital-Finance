\begin{abstract}
This paper examines the impact of high-frequency trading (HFT) on price information, trader behavior, and market efficiency. By leveraging empirical data and advanced econometric models, we analyze the role of HFT in modern financial markets and its implications for regulatory policies.
\end{abstract}
