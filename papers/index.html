Project Overview

The Narrative Digital Finance project (SNSF Grant IZCOZ0_213370) investigates the intersection of natural language processing and quantitative finance across four research blocks.

Central Finding (Block 2)

Sentiment LAGS structural breaks by 5 days - contradicting the conventional assumption that sentiment leads market movements. This finding has significant implications for sentiment-based trading strategies.
