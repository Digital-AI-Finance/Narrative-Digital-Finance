Project Overview

The Narrative Digital Finance project explores how textual information from news, social media, and corporate communications influences financial markets. We develop machine learning frameworks that integrate natural language processing with traditional quantitative methods to improve market prediction and risk management.

Work Packages

1Data Infrastructure

Building comprehensive datasets combining market data with textual narratives from multiple sources.

2NLP Methods

Developing finance-specific NLP models for sentiment analysis, topic modeling, and narrative extraction.

3Market Dynamics

Analyzing lead-lag relationships between narratives and market movements across asset classes.

4Integrated ML Framework

Ex-ante structural break detection combining NLP, change point detection, and ensemble methods.
