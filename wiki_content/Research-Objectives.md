# Research Objectives

## Overall Objectives

The project aims to develop a comprehensive framework that utilizes advanced machine learning and NLP techniques to:

- **Predict market outcomes**
- **Detect asset price bubbles**
- **Identify structural breaks**
- **Analyze diverse data sources** including financial data and narrative content from text, speech, and multimedia

---

## Specific Aims

### Aim 1: Econometric Model Validation
Validate and refine existing econometric models using real-world financial data

### Aim 2: Narrative Analysis Integration
Integrate narrative analysis to understand and predict market behaviors and asset price dynamics

### Aim 3: AI/ML Framework Development
Create a multidimensional AI and ML framework that enhances the detection of market anomalies and forecasts financial trends

---

## Methodology

### Data Collection and Processing
- Stock prices
- Macroeconomic indicators
- Textual content from the web

### Text Mining and NLP Techniques
- Sentiment analysis
- Narrative structure analysis
- Impact assessment on market movements

### AI Model Development
- Combine traditional financial analysis with narrative insights
- Predict market changes
- Detect structural breaks

---

## Expected Outcomes

| Outcome | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Enhanced Models | Improved prediction accuracy for asset price bubbles and structural breaks |
| Novel Datasets | New measurement techniques for market narrative analysis |
| Practical Tools | Applications for asset management and regulatory bodies |
